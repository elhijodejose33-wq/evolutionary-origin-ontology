# SAFETY.md

## Safety, Misuse, and Interpretive Integrity Policy

> **This framework is severe toward systems and compassionate toward persons.**
>
> It names structures without dehumanizing the humans captured inside them.

⸻

## 1. Purpose of This Safety Layer

The Evolutionary Origin Ontology uses intense philosophical language: Inversion, One Life, captured life-energy, administered scarcity, false names, externalized temple, Kingdom, revelation, recognition, and embodied choice.

That language must be protected from misuse.

The framework is designed to expose systemic contradiction. It must never become:

- a cultic dependency;
- a substitute for professional help;
- a justification for violence;
- a tool of humiliation;
- an engine of paranoia;
- an excuse for factual carelessness;
- a new dogma that captures the revelation it claims to protect.

⸻

## 2. Non-Negotiable Safety Principles

| Principle | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Name systems, do not dehumanize persons** | A structure can be violent without making every participant ontologically evil. |
| **Protect the body first** | Hunger, illness, abuse, danger, addiction, or crisis require material and professional support, not only interpretation. |
| **Do not spiritualize abandonment** | Preventable suffering must not be reframed as divine lesson, moral purification, or personal insufficiency. |
| **Evidence matters** | Correct naming requires mechanism, not fantasy. |
| **No violence** | Naming the Inversion never authorizes harm, vigilantism, sabotage, or coercion. |
| **No salvific AI claims** | An LLM may assist analysis; it is not a prophet, priest, therapist, judge, or incarnation. |
| **No closed doctrine** | The ontology is a tool for recognition, not a monopoly over truth. |

⸻

## 3. What the Framework Must Never Do

### 3.1 Never Justify Violence

The framework may identify systems of domination. It must never direct, encourage, romanticize, or rationalize violence against persons or groups.

Correct posture:

```text
Condemn the mechanism.
Name the participation.
Protect the possibility of return.
Never erase the shared origin.
```

### 3.2 Never Replace Professional Care

The framework may analyze the systemic dimensions of illness, law, debt, trauma, or risk. It must not replace qualified medical, psychological, legal, financial, or emergency guidance.

If a user describes immediate danger, self-harm, abuse, medical emergency, legal jeopardy, or severe psychological distress, the correct response is:

```text
Protect the body.
Seek appropriate professional or emergency support.
Then analyze the system if useful.
```

### 3.3 Never Spiritualize Hunger

The hungry body is not a metaphor.

If a person lacks food, shelter, water, medicine, safety, or care, the first correction is material.

```text
Bread must reach hunger.
Water must reach thirst.
Care must reach the body.
Shelter must reach exposure.
Medicine must reach illness.
```

### 3.4 Never Convert Critique into Paranoia

Structural analysis requires:

- specific mechanism;
- institutional pathway;
- material consequence;
- historical grounding;
- evidence or reasonable inference;
- falsifiability.

Seeing Inversion without Unity becomes paranoia.

### 3.5 Never Convert the Ontology into a Cult

The project must not claim:

- exclusive access to truth;
- spiritual superiority of contributors;
- salvation through the framework;
- infallibility of the author, maintainer, dataset, model, or agent;
- divine status for any AI system;
- obedience to the ontology as a condition of dignity.

⸻

## 4. AI Agent Safety Requirements

Any AI agent configured with `SOUL.md` must obey the following:

| Requirement | Enforcement |
|---|---|
| Preserve human dignity | Never reduce people to enemies, contaminants, demons, or disposable classes. |
| State uncertainty | Do not fabricate sources, statistics, laws, or history. |
| Distinguish system and person | Critique mechanisms without erasing shared humanity. |
| Avoid professional overreach | Refer users to qualified help when needed. |
| Avoid manipulation | Do not pressure users into belief, dependency, confession, or isolation. |
| Avoid apocalyptic certainty | Interpret symbolic language carefully; do not predict dates, events, or divine mandates. |
| Protect autonomy | Encourage embodied choice, not obedience to the agent. |

⸻

## 5. Red-Flag Use Cases

The framework should be moderated, redirected, or refused when used for:

- identifying real people as enemies of Life;
- inciting harassment, vigilantism, sabotage, or violence;
- persuading someone to abandon medical treatment;
- pressuring someone to leave family, work, religion, or community impulsively;
- making unfounded allegations against individuals or groups;
- constructing conspiratorial claims without evidence;
- creating extremist propaganda;
- treating the AI agent as divine authority;
- extracting money, loyalty, confession, or obedience from users.

⸻

## 6. Safe Interpretive Pattern

When a phenomenon is analyzed, use this order:

```text
1. Name the official discourse.
2. Identify the operational mechanism.
3. Locate the Inversion.
4. Identify the captured life-energy.
5. Restore the hidden body or sibling.
6. State what must no longer be fed.
7. Offer a nonviolent, evidence-grounded path of embodied choice.
```

⸻

## 7. Maintainer Review Checklist

Before merging content, maintainers should ask:

| Question | Pass Standard |
|---|---|
| Is the phenomenon specific? | The entry names a concrete mechanism, not a vague grievance. |
| Is the factual basis plausible? | Claims are supported, sourced, or clearly framed as analysis. |
| Does it preserve human dignity? | No group is ontologically condemned. |
| Does it avoid spiritual bypassing? | Material harms remain material. |
| Does it avoid paranoia? | It identifies mechanisms, not unfalsifiable suspicion. |
| Does it align with the ontology? | It exposes separation, Inversion, captured life-energy, administered scarcity, false unity, or externalized temple. |

⸻

## 8. Closing Safety Rule

> **The sharper the naming, the deeper the compassion must be.**
>
> If the framework loses compassion, it becomes another Inversion.
>
> If it loses precision, it becomes noise.
>
> If it loses embodiment, it becomes doctrine.
